Perfect Meatloaf and Mashed Potatoes: A Comfort Dinner to Remember

Molly Dupont Published on May 25, 2026
15 min prep 60 min cook 6 servings

This classic meatloaf paired with creamy mashed potatoes is pure comfort food. It's hearty, satisfying, and perfect for family dinners on chilly nights!

There's something so nostalgic about a home-cooked meatloaf and mashed potatoes dinner. I remember my mom making this dish on chilly evenings, filling the house with the comforting aroma of baked meat and savory spices. This perfect meatloaf is juicy and flavorful, paired with creamy mashed potatoes that just melt in your mouth. It's a dish that brings everyone together, and trust me, it's a hit at family dinners!

What You Need

For this meatloaf, you'll need ground beef, which gives it a hearty flavor. I like using a mix of breadcrumbs and seasonings to keep it moist. If you don’t have breadcrumbs, crushed crackers work in a pinch! The mashed potatoes are made creamy with butter and heavy cream, but feel free to lighten it up with milk if you prefer.

How to Make It

Making this meatloaf is straightforward. Combine all the ingredients, shape it into a loaf, and bake! While that's happening, boil your potatoes until tender, mash them up with butter and cream, and you've got a comforting side. It’s a simple process that yields a deliciously satisfying meal.

Here's the thing - This meatloaf is wonderful on its own, but you can also dress it up with a side of green beans or a fresh salad. Want to make it spicier? Add some diced jalapeños to the mix! And if you have leftovers, they make for a great meatloaf sandwich the next day.

Tips & Notes

For a really moist meatloaf, don’t skip the milk! A common mistake is overmixing the meat; gently combine the ingredients. Leftover meatloaf is fantastic in sandwiches, just slice and enjoy on your favorite bread!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How To Do It

  1. Step 1

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Step 2

    In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, milk, ketchup, egg, salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce until well combined.

  3. Step 3

    Transfer the meat mixture to a loaf pan and shape it into a loaf. Spread a little extra ketchup on top for a glaze.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (70°C).

  5. Step 5

    While the meatloaf is baking, place the cubed potatoes in a large pot of salted water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender, about 15-20 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add butter, heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Mash until smooth and creamy.

  7. Step 7

    Serve slices of meatloaf alongside a generous scoop of mashed potatoes.
