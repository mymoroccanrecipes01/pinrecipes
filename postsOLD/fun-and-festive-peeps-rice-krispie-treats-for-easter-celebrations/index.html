Fun and Festive Peeps Rice Krispie Treats for Easter Celebrations

These Peeps Rice Krispie Treats are a playful twist on a classic favorite, perfect for Easter. The colorful marshmallow Peeps add a fun pop and extra sweetness. Great for sharing at spring parties or family gatherings!

Pro Tips

Use a buttered spatula or wax paper to press the cereal mixture into the pan.
This prevents sticking and allows you to press the treats evenly without tearing or compressing the mixture too much, which keeps the texture light and chewy.
Don’t overheat the marshmallows when melting.
High heat can cause marshmallows to burn or become grainy, ruining the smooth texture needed to bind the cereal properly.
Add Peeps only after pressing the base mixture into the pan.
Pressing Peeps into the warm base helps them stick without melting completely, preserving their fun shapes and colors.
Cut treats with a warm, clean knife for neat edges.
Wiping the knife between cuts prevents sticky marshmallow residue from tearing the treats, resulting in cleaner squares.
Store treats in an airtight container at room temperature.
Refrigeration can make the cereal hard and chewy rather than crispy, so room temperature storage keeps the texture just right.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Overheating marshmallows

Why it happens People often turn up the heat to melt marshmallows faster, thinking it saves time.

Fix Melt marshmallows on low heat with constant stirring. This prevents burning and keeps the mixture smooth and glossy.

Pressing the mixture too hard

Why it happens Some think compacting the treats tightly will make them hold better.

Fix Press gently but firmly to keep treats light and chewy. Over-compressing makes them dense and tough.

Adding Peeps too early

Why it happens In a hurry, Peeps are sometimes mixed in with the cereal mixture.

Fix Add Peeps on top after pressing the base. This keeps their shape and color intact.

Cutting treats too soon

Why it happens Impatience leads to slicing before the treats set.

Fix Let treats cool at room temperature for at least 30 minutes to firm up before cutting.

Variations

Chocolate Drizzle Peeps Treats

After the treats have cooled, drizzle melted dark or milk chocolate over the top for a richer flavor and extra festive look. It adds a nice contrast to the sweet marshmallows.

Note: Allow the chocolate to set at room temperature or chill briefly; avoid applying chocolate when treats are warm to prevent melting.

Peanut Butter Marshmallow Base

Mix 1/2 cup peanut butter into the melted marshmallow mixture before adding cereal for a nutty twist. This adds protein and a savory balance to the sweetness.

Note: Peanut butter thickens the mixture, so stir quickly and press firmly into the pan to ensure good binding.

Vegan Version with Coconut Oil and Vegan Marshmallows

Use coconut oil instead of butter and substitute with vegan marshmallows to make these treats plant-based. The texture stays similar but with a subtle coconut flavor.

Note: Vegan marshmallows melt differently, so melt gently and stir continuously to avoid graininess.

Add Rainbow Sprinkles

Sprinkle colorful jimmies or nonpareils over the treats right after pressing the mixture into the pan for added fun and texture. Great for kids’ parties.

Note: Press sprinkles lightly so they stick but don’t sink into the marshmallow base.

Storage & Reheating

Room Temperature

Keep treats in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Covering tightly prevents marshmallow from drying out and keeps the cereal crispy.

Refrigerator

Refrigeration is not recommended as it makes the cereal hard and chewy. If refrigerated, let treats come to room temperature before serving for better texture.

Freezer

Freeze treats wrapped tightly in plastic wrap and stored in a freezer-safe container for up to 1 month. Thaw overnight in the fridge and then bring to room temperature before serving.

Reheating

No reheating needed. If treats become too firm, warming briefly in a low oven (250°F) for 2-3 minutes softens them without melting the Peeps.

Make Ahead

Prepare the marshmallow base and cereal mixture 1-2 days ahead and store in an airtight container. Add Peeps just before serving to keep their fresh look and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use regular marshmallows instead of mini marshmallows?
Yes, but mini marshmallows melt more evenly and quickly, which helps create a smooth coating. If using regular marshmallows, chop them into smaller pieces before melting to ensure even melting and prevent clumps.
What if my Peeps start to melt too much when pressed on the treats?
Peeps soften when warm but shouldn’t melt completely. To avoid this, let the Rice Krispies mixture cool slightly before adding Peeps, and press gently just enough to stick them on without squashing.
Can I make these treats gluten-free?
Absolutely! Use gluten-free crispy rice cereal to keep the treats safe for gluten-sensitive eaters. Just double-check the marshmallows and Peeps ingredients to ensure they’re gluten-free as well.
How do I keep the treats from sticking to the pan?
Grease your pan well with butter or nonstick spray before adding the mixture. You can also line the pan with parchment paper for easier removal and cleaner edges.
Can I add other mix-ins like chocolate chips or nuts?
Yes, but add them after mixing the cereal with marshmallow, just before pressing into the pan. Keep mix-ins light to avoid weighing down the treats and compromising their structure.
What’s the best way to store leftover treats?
Store leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Avoid refrigeration to keep the cereal crisp. If you need longer storage, freeze well wrapped and thaw before serving.

Ingredients

Approximately 12 large squares

  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal, crispy and fresh
  • 4 cups mini marshmallows, regular
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 package Peeps marshmallow chicks or bunnies, assorted colors

Instructions

Step 1
Start by melting the butter in a large saucepan over low heat. This gentle heat prevents the butter from browning, which can affect flavor. Once the butter is melted, add the mini marshmallows and stir continuously. The key here is patience — keep the heat low and stir until the marshmallows are completely melted and smooth. Rushing this step or using high heat can cause the marshmallows to burn or become grainy, which changes the texture of your treats.
⏱ Time: 5 minutes
✓ Doneness: Marshmallow mixture is glossy and smooth with no lumps
Step 2
Remove the saucepan from heat and immediately add the Rice Krispies cereal. Stir quickly but gently to coat all the cereal evenly with the marshmallow mixture. It's important to mix swiftly to prevent the marshmallow from setting before the cereal is fully coated. If you wait too long, the treats will be dry and crumbly instead of soft and chewy.
⏱ Time: 2 minutes
✓ Doneness: All cereal pieces are shiny and sticky, with no dry spots
Step 3
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan with butter or nonstick spray. Transfer the cereal mixture into the pan and press it down firmly but gently with a buttered spatula or wax paper. Pressing evenly ensures the treats hold together without becoming too compressed, which keeps them chewy instead of hard.
⏱ Time: 3 minutes
✓ Doneness: Mixture is evenly spread and compacted without cracking
Step 4
Arrange the Peeps marshmallow chicks or bunnies on top of the pressed cereal mixture. Be sure to space them evenly for a colorful presentation. Gently press each Peep into the treats so they stick but don’t melt completely. This step adds visual appeal and a fun texture contrast.
⏱ Time: 3 minutes
✓ Doneness: Peeps are slightly embedded but maintain their shape and color
Step 5
Allow the treats to cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes to set. This resting time helps the marshmallow bind the cereal and Peeps without becoming sticky or too soft. Avoid refrigerating right away as it can make the treats hard and less enjoyable to bite.
⏱ Time: 30 minutes
✓ Doneness: Treats are firm to the touch but still soft and chewy inside
Step 6
Once cooled, cut the treats into squares or rectangles using a sharp knife. To get clean cuts, wipe the knife with a warm, damp cloth between slices. This prevents sticking and keeps edges neat.
⏱ Time: 5 minutes
✓ Doneness: Clean, even squares with Peeps intact on top
Step 7
Serve immediately or store in an airtight container at room temperature. These treats are best enjoyed within a day or two to keep the Peeps fresh and the cereal crispy. If you want to make them ahead, you can prepare the base and add Peeps just before serving to keep that fresh look.
⏱ Time: N/A
✓ Doneness: Treats remain fresh and Peeps colorful when stored properly
