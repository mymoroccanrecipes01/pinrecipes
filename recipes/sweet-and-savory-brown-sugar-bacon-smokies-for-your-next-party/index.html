Brown Sugar Bacon Smokies: The Ultimate Appetizer

Molly Dupont Published on March 1, 2026
10 min prep 20 min cook 6 servings

These Brown Sugar Bacon Smokies are a game-changer for any gathering! With just three simple ingredients, you’re gonna whip these up in no time. Perfect for game day or holiday gatherings. Seriously, you gotta try this!

The aroma of crispy bacon combined with sweet brown sugar fills the kitchen, making it impossible to resist. What I Learned The Hard Way

First time I tried making these, I went too heavy on the sugar. DISASTER. The sugar burnt and turned into a sticky mess. Now I know to keep an eye on them and maybe use a bit less sugar to get that sweet spot of caramelization.

Essential Ingredients & Smart Substitutions

  • Bacon: Using a quality bacon, like Kerrygold, makes a huge difference in flavor. Look for thick-cut bacon for a satisfying bite.
  • Brown Sugar: This adds that sweet, caramelized touch, but you could also use coconut sugar for a healthier option.
  • Cocktail Sausages: These are key for the smokies. You can swap them out for turkey sausages if you’re looking for a lighter version.
  • Equipment: A baking sheet lined with parchment paper is essential. It keeps everything from sticking and makes cleanup a breeze.

Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions

Preparation phase:
Start by cutting your bacon and preheating the oven. This takes just a few minutes. You can even wrap the sausages ahead of time and store them in the fridge.
Cooking process:
Wrap, roll, and bake! It’s that simple. Bake until the bacon is crispy, about 20 minutes. Keep an eye on them towards the end to ensure they don’t burn.
Finishing touches:
Once done, let them cool slightly before serving. They’ll be hot and sticky, so be careful not to burn your mouth!

Flavor Boosters & Customization Ideas

  • Herbs: Try adding some fresh rosemary or thyme for an aromatic twist. Just sprinkle them on before baking.
  • Spices: A dash of cayenne or smoked paprika can give these a subtle kick. Start with a pinch and adjust to taste.
  • Upgrades: Swap out regular sausages for spicy ones or even add a slice of jalapeño for a surprise!
  • Technique variations: Instead of baking, you could grill these for a smoky flavor. Just watch the sugar so it doesn't burn!

Perfect Pairings & Serving Suggestions

  • Side dishes: These pair nicely with a fresh green salad or a bowl of creamy coleslaw. The crunch balances the sweetness of the smokies.
  • Beverage pairings: Serve with a cold beer or a fruity cocktail. The sweetness complements the smokiness beautifully.
  • Bread options: Fresh dinner rolls or crusty baguette are great for soaking up any leftover glaze.
  • Occasion ideas: From tailgating to holiday parties, these smokies fit right in. They’re also a hit at family gatherings.
  • Presentation tips: Arrange them on a platter with toothpicks for easy snacking. Add some fresh parsley for a pop of color.
  • Temperature serving note: Best served warm, but they’re also tasty at room temperature!

Creative Variations to Try

  • Gluten-Free: Use gluten-free sausages and check the bacon for gluten content. Easy swap!
  • Dairy-Free: Most bacon is dairy-free, but double-check brands. This recipe is naturally dairy-free!
  • Spicy Version: Add hot sauce to the brown sugar mix for a spicy-sweet combo.
  • Different Proteins: Try wrapping chicken bites instead of sausages. Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.

Pro Chef Secrets

  • Restaurant technique: Try marinating the sausages in a little soy sauce before wrapping for extra umami flavor.
  • Ingredient prep secret: Soak the bacon in cold water for a few minutes before cutting to make it easier to handle.
  • Timing strategy: Keep an eye on the last few minutes in the oven; the sugar can go from perfect to burnt quickly.
  • Presentation hack: Serve on a wooden board for that rustic vibe. It adds to the aesthetic!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, cut into thirds
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 package cocktail sausages (about 14 ounces)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). You want it nice and hot for those smokies.

  2. Step 2

    Wrap each cocktail sausage in a piece of bacon. Seriously, just wrap it tight enough to hold together but not too tight.

  3. Step 3

    Roll those wrapped beauties in brown sugar. You can be generous here; it’s gonna be sweet!

  4. Step 4

    Place them seam-side down on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. This helps with the cleanup later.

  5. Step 5

    Bake for about 20 minutes until the bacon is crispy and the sugar has caramelized. Keep an eye on them - you want golden, not burnt!

  6. Step 6

    Let them cool for a few minutes before serving. I mean, trust me, you don’t want to burn your tongue!

Tips & Notes

Quick heads up: These smokies are seriously addictive! Here are some pro tips to nail this recipe: - Use high-quality bacon like Kerrygold for that extra flavor. It makes a difference! - If you're short on time, you can prep these ahead of time. Just wrap the sausages and refrigerate. Bake them when you're ready to serve. - Want a little kick? Sprinkle some cayenne pepper over the bacon before baking. - Don't skip the parchment paper! It makes cleanup a breeze. - If you have leftovers (which is rare), store them in an airtight container and reheat in the oven to keep them crispy.
