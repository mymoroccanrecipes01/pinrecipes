Quick Air Fryer Egg & Cheese Toast for Busy Mornings

Molly Dupont Published on March 1, 2026
10 min prep 5 min cook 2 servings

Essential Ingredients & Smart Substitutions

Main ingredient: I love using fresh eggs because they make the toast so fluffy. Look for large, organic eggs if you can. They really do taste better!

  • Key seasonings: Salt and pepper are a must. I sometimes add a pinch of garlic powder for extra flavor.
  • Supporting ingredients: The bread is crucial! I go for whole grain, but any bread you love works.

Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions

Preparation is key! Start by gathering all your ingredients and preheating your air fryer. It takes about 5 minutes to prep, so measure everything out. You can even whisk the eggs ahead of time if you're in a rush.

Cooking process:
Cook the bread for about 2 minutes before adding the eggs. This way, the bread gets crispy while the egg cooks. You’ll see the edges start to brown.
Finishing touches:
Once it’s done, let it cool for a minute before slicing. Serve it with some fresh fruit or a side salad for a complete meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (I use Kerrygold cheddar)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for spreading (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    First, preheat your air fryer to 350°F (175°C). You want it nice and warm, like a cozy hug for your toast.

  2. Step 2

    While that’s heating up, crack your eggs into a bowl and whisk them lightly. Add a pinch of salt and pepper - you know, to taste.

  3. Step 3

    Next, grab those slices of bread and spread a thin layer of butter on each side. Trust me, it adds that golden crunch that’s hard to resist.

  4. Step 4

    Pop the bread in the air fryer for about 2 minutes on one side until it starts to get crispy. Keep an eye on it, because my air fryer likes to run hot.

  5. Step 5

    Now, flip the bread, pour the whisked eggs on top, and sprinkle the cheese over. Air fry for another 3 minutes until the cheese is melty and the egg looks set.

  6. Step 6

    Remove it carefully (it’s hot!), slice in half, and dig in... you won't regret it!

Tips & Notes

So, here are some pro tips for your Air Fryer Egg & Cheese Toast: 1. **Temperature matters:** Keep your air fryer at 350°F. Too hot, and the bread burns before the egg cooks. 2. **Don’t skip the butter:** It gives a beautiful golden color and adds flavor. 3. **Use fresh eggs:** They’ll give a fluffier texture. If you can, go for organic! 4. **Experiment with cheese:** I love Kerrygold, but try mozzarella or pepper jack for a twist! 5. **Storage:** If you have leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days. Reheat in the air fryer for a quick breakfast redo.
